Ingredients:

  • Macaroni (boiled)
  • Tomato (chopped)
  • Onion (sliced)
  • Green bell pepper (capsicum, chopped)
  • Corn
  • Carrot (chopped)
  • Eggs (2 - scrambled or boiled)
  • Shredded boiled chicken
  • Macaroni masala (readymade or homemade)
  • Red chili powder
  • Coriander powder
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Garlic paste
  • Adrak (ginger) paste
  • Ketchup (optional, after cooking)

Steps to Cook:

  1. Boil macaroni and keep it aside.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and add garlic and adrak paste.
  3. Add onions and saut√© until golden brown.
  4. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until soft.
  5. Now add capsicum, corn, and carrots. Cook for 5 minutes.
  6. Add shredded boiled chicken and mix well.
  7. Add macaroni masala, red chili powder, coriander powder, and salt.
  8. Mix all ingredients and cook on medium flame.
  9. Add the scrambled or chopped boiled eggs.
  10. Finally, add the boiled macaroni and mix everything properly.
  11. Cook for 5-7 more minutes. You can add a little ketchup if desired.
